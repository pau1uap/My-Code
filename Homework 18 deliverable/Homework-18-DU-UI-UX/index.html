The Story

Paul Becker has always had a curious mind. Once in 6th grade his teacher had a conversation with him about asking too many questions in class because it distracted from her lesson plan. She told him, “Paul, never stop asking questions just make sure the ones you’re asking are the right ones to get the answers you seek.”

This helped to give a scope to the way Paul thought about the world around him and the how and why things function the way they do. With this mentality Paul gravitated to the arts and design as a way to visualize and communicate solutions to his curiosity.
